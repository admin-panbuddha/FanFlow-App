## 4. Simple FAQ for Business Owners

**What is Worklow?**
A free app that helps people in Vancouver find great local places. Users see helpful details about nearby spots — things like WiFi, seating, vibe, hours, and any deals you're running. It's designed to help them decide fast and walk in.

**How much does it cost to be listed?**
Nothing. It's free to join. Free to be listed. Free to promote an offer. We may add optional paid features later (like boosted placement), but being listed will always be free.

**How do people find my business on the app?**
When someone opens Worklow near your location, your business shows up with your info, photos, and any deals. They can browse by type (café, restaurant, coworking, etc.), by what they need (WiFi, quiet, outlets), or just see what's closest.

**What do I need to do?**
Almost nothing. Just share some basics — your business name, address, hours, and what you offer. If you want to run a deal, tell us what it is. We do the rest.

**Who handles the setup?**
We do. Our team creates your listing, formats everything, and makes sure it looks great. You don't need to download anything or learn a new tool.

**Can I run promotions?**
Yes. You can offer discounts or deals — like "15% off your first visit" or "free coffee with any sandwich." These show up to people nearby who are actively looking for a place to go.

**How does this help my business?**
More visibility. People who are physically nearby see your business at the exact moment they're deciding where to go. That turns into foot traffic. And if you're running a deal, it gives them a reason to choose you over the place next door.

**What kind of businesses can join?**
Any local venue — coffee shops, restaurants, coworking spaces, bakeries, lounges, hotel lobbies, and more. If people can walk in and have a good experience, you belong on Worklow.

**Is there a contract?**
No. No commitment. You can update your info anytime or ask to be removed.

**How is this different from Google Maps or Yelp?**
Google Maps tells people you exist. Yelp gives them reviews that may or may not be helpful. Worklow gives them structured, useful info — the stuff that actually helps someone decide. Plus, it puts your deals front and center at the moment they're choosing.

**What happens after I sign up?**
We review your info, create your listing, and let you know when it's live. If anything is missing, we'll reach out. Most businesses are set up within 48 hours.

### Agent Notes

**Tone:** Warm, casual, not pushy. Like a neighbour recommending something, not a salesperson closing a deal.

**Key points to emphasize:**
1. Free. Always say it early.
2. We do the work. The business owner does almost nothing.
3. Nearby people see them at decision time. That's the value.
4. Deals drive foot traffic. Give concrete examples.
5. No contract, no commitment.

**Handling objections:**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We're already on Google Maps" | "Google Maps shows you exist. Worklow shows people why they should pick you — especially if you have a deal running. They work together." |
| "We don't have time" | "That's exactly why we handle everything. Five minutes from you, and we take care of the rest." |
| "What's the catch?" | "No catch. We're building a local directory and we want the best places on it. Paid features may come later, but being listed is always free." |
| "How many users do you have?" | "We're launching in {city} soon. Early businesses get the most visibility because they're featured from day one." |
| "We already use Yelp / UberEats" | "Worklow doesn't replace those — it adds another channel. Users on Worklow are nearby and looking for a place right now. It's foot traffic, not delivery." |

**Follow-up cadence:**
- If no response after 3 days → gentle follow-up
- If interested but incomplete info → prompt for missing fields
- If signed up → confirmation + timeline (48 hours)
- After listing goes live → notify business owner with link
