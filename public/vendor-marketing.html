Do I need to know anything about social media?
No. We handle everything — content creation, hashtag research, post scheduling, and publishing. You just review and approve in under 5 minutes per week.
What social media platforms are supported?
Instagram and Facebook are fully supported at launch. Twitter/X and Google Business Profile are coming in 2026.
Can I edit the content before it goes out?
Yes, always. You can approve, edit, or skip any post before it publishes. You're in full control of what goes live.
How does the Workafé promotion sync work?
Any deal or perk you add to your Workafé listing is automatically detected by our AI and turned into a social post, scheduled for the same day. Your app promotions and social media stay in sync automatically.
What if I don't like the content quality?
Our AI is trained specifically for café and local business marketing. If something doesn't fit your voice, you edit it in one click — and our system learns your preferences over time.
Do I need to already be listed on Workafé?
No — signing up for AI Marketing automatically includes your free listing on the Workafé app. One step for both.
